About Geethika

Geethika, known creatively as Geethika Artistry, is a self-taught artist based in Vizianagaram, Andhra Pradesh, India. Her work reflects a deep passion for human expression and individuality, brought to life through delicate pencil sketches, freestyle illustrations, and graphite art enhanced with subtle touches of watercolor.

Driven by curiosity and creativity, Geethika began her artistic journey independently, developing her skills through continuous practice and personal exploration rather than formal training. This self-guided path has allowed her to cultivate a unique artistic voice that blends realism with emotional depth.

Her artworks focus primarily on live portraits, capturing not just physical resemblance but also the personality and emotions of her subjects. Each piece is carefully crafted with attention to detail, texture, and shading, making every portrait a meaningful and personal creation.

Geethika believes that art is more than just visual beauty — it is a way of storytelling. Through her pencil and graphite works, she transforms simple lines into expressive forms that resonate with viewers and preserve memories in artistic form.

Currently, she offers paid live portrait commissions, working closely with clients to create customized pieces that reflect their vision and story. Her work is ideal for personal keepsakes, gifts, and special moments captured through art.

With a growing presence on social media, Geethika Artistry continues to reach art lovers and clients who appreciate handcrafted, soulful portrait art rooted in simplicity and elegance.
